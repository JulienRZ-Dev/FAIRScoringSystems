Highlighting all occurrences of an OPL element

When you select an OPL element in a model, all occurrences of the element are highlighted.

In this screen, there are two occurrences of the element TasksNotBreakable highlighted. The blue vertical line indicates the scope of the subject to statement, and the + symbols indicate that the forall statements have been collapsed. Click on + to expand the statements.
