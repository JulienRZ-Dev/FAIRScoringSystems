maxint

Keyword for the largest positive integer in OPL

Purpose

OPL keyword to represent the largest positive integer.

context
Model files (.mod)

Syntax

LiteralExpression: "(integer-literal)"
                  | "(float-literal)"
                  | "(boolean-literal)"
                  | "(string-literal)"
                  | "infinity"
                  | "maxint"

Description

The integer constant maxint represents the largest positive integer available in OPL (231 – 1 for 32 bit machines and 263 – 1 for 64 bit machines).

OPL provides a subset of the integers ranging from -maxint to maxint as a basic data type.

Note that expressions involving large integers may produce overflow.

Example

dvar int x in 0..maxint;
