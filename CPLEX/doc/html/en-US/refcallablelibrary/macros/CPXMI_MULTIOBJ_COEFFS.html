CPXMI_MULTIOBJ_COEFFS

1062 Detected objective coefficients that may be suited for multiobjective optimization

#define CPXMI_MULTIOBJ_COEFFS 1062
A model having objective coefficients that are separated by two or more orders of magnitude, or having objective coefficients in a given range that are all identical, may be a good candidate for reformulation as a multiobjective model.
