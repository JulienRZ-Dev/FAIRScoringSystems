CPX_NO_ABSTOL_CHANGE

#define CPX_NO_ABSTOL_CHANGE NAN

Description: When the attributes of an objective are changed in a multiobjective optimization problem, pass in CPX_NO_ABSTOL_CHANGE to specify that no changes are need to be made to the absolute tolerance of the objective.
