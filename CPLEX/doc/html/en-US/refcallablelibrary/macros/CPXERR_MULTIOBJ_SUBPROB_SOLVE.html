CPXERR_MULTIOBJ_SUBPROB_SOLVE

1300 Failure to solve multi-objective subproblem

#define CPXERR_MULTIOBJ_SUBPROB_SOLVE 1300
CPXmultiobjopt failed to solve one of the subproblems in a multi-objective optimization. Query the individual sub-problem statuses for more information about the cause of the failure.
